Build challenges

AWS infrastructure changes
A shift in AWS broke deployment, causing read errors and inaccurate (sometimes negative) scores.
Backend timeouts
Some policy pages caused the lambda function to time out, returning negative scores instead of failing cleanly.
Outdated dependencies
Audited and updated packages across the codebase to reduce security and compatibility risk.
Cloud provider lock-in
Tested Google Cloud as a working backup in case AWS becomes unreliable again.

FAQ

Does it read the whole policy?
Yes, the full text, not just keywords.
Can I trust the score?
Treat it as a strong starting point, not a legal verdict.
Can I prioritize what matters to me?
Yes, weight any of the 10 categories yourself.
What's "readability" measure?
How hard the policy is to read, separate from its privacy score.
